Questions


  1. What types of vehicles are available for rent?

    Our car rental website offers a wide range of vehicles, including compact cars, sedans, SUVs, minivans, and luxury cars. You can choose the vehicle that best suits your needs and preferences.

  2. What is the rental cost per day/week/month?

    The rental cost depends on various factors, such as the type of vehicle, rental duration, pickup/dropoff location, and optional extras. You can check the current rental rates on our website or contact our customer service for a customized quote.

  3. What are the rental requirements, such as age, driver's license, and insurance?

    To rent a car from us, you need to meet the minimum age requirement (usually 21 or 25 years old), have a valid driver's license issued by your country of residence, and provide proof of insurance. We also require a security deposit, which can be paid with a credit card or cash.

  4. Are there any restrictions on where I can take the car or how many miles I can drive?

    Our rental agreements usually allow unlimited mileage within the country or state of rental, but may have restrictions on cross-border travel or specific routes. Please read the terms and conditions carefully or ask our customer service for clarification.

  5. What happens if the car breaks down or is involved in an accident?

    In case of a breakdown or accident, please contact our roadside assistance or emergency hotline immediately. We will provide you with instructions on how to proceed and may arrange a replacement vehicle or tow service if necessary. Please note that additional fees may apply for certain situations, such as negligence or violation of the rental agreement.
